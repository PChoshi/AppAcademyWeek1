© Carol Zoe Mahlangu – A Heart in Every Dish

My name is Carol Mahlangu, and cooking has been a part of me for as long as I can remember. From a young age, I found joy in the kitchen—watching, learning, and eventually creating my own dishes with love and curiosity. What started as a childhood passion has grown into a lifelong calling. Food is more than just nourishment to me—it’s a story, a memory, a celebration. I cook with heart, drawing from tradition, creativity, and a desire to bring people together. Whether it’s a hearty stew, soft steamed bread, or a beautifully plated salad, every dish I make is crafted with care and intention. Over the years, I’ve refined my craft not just through experience, but through listening to those I cook for. I’ve served meals at private events, created comforting food for families, and developed signature sauces and seasoning blends that elevate simple meals into unforgettable experiences.
